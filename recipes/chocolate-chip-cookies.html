Ingredients

  • 2-1/2 cups flour
  • 1 tsp salt
  • 1 tsp baking soda
  • 1 cup butter-flavor Crisco
  • 3/4 cup sugar
  • 3/4 cup brown sugar
  • 1 tsp vanilla extract
  • 2 large eggs
  • 1 package choloclate chips

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 350 degrees F.
  2. Stir together flour, salt, and baking soda.
  3. In a separate bowl. mix together the butter-flavor Crisco, sugar, brown sugar, and vanilla.
  4. Add eggs into the wet mixture.
  5. Stir the dry mixture into the wet mixture amd add the chocolate chips.
  6. Form cookie dough into balls (1 tbsp).
  7. Bake for 7-9 minutes.
