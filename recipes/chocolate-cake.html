Ingredients

  • 2 cups sugar
  • 1-3/4 cups flour
  • 3/4 cup cocoa powder
  • 1-1/2 tsp baking powder
  • 1-1/2 tsp baking soda
  • 1 tsp salt
  • 2 eggs
  • 1 cup milk
  • 1/2 cup vegetable oil
  • 2 tsp vanilla extract
  • 1 cup boiling water

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 350 degrees F. Grease and flour two 9-in round baking pans.
  2. Combine dry ingredients in large bowl. Add eggs, milk, oil, and vanilla; beat on medium speed for 2 minutes. Stir in boiling water (batter will be thin). Pour into pans.
  3. Bake 30-35 minutes or unitl wooden pick inserted in center comes out clean. Cool 10 minutes; remove from pans to wire racks. Cool completely.
